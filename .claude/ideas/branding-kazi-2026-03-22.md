# Branding Decision — HeyKazi
**Date**: 2026-03-22 (updated 2026-04-01)

## Product Name: HeyKazi
- **Origin**: "Hey" + Kazi (Swahili: "work") — conversational, ties into the in-app AI assistant
- **Domain**: heykazi.com (primary, available as of 2026-04-01)
- **Why "Hey" prefix**: Natural voice command ("Hey Kazi, show me unbilled time"), works as both brand and AI assistant invocation, .com available
- **Previous name**: Kazi — kazi.com taken, kazi.io taken and 5x more expensive
- **Rejected alternatives**:
  - Sebenza (Zulu: work) — Chris Reeve Knives brand collision, SA startup namespace crowded
  - Lumino (Latin: light) — clean but not African
  - Khanya (Xhosa: shine) — beautiful but khanya.co.za active site
  - Vutha, Modiri considered
  - Thuma (Zulu: send) — thuma.com blank/parked, thuma.io available but .io 5x cost
  - Kazini (Swahili: at the workplace), Ekazi (Zulu: at work), GoKazi, Dala, Nala — all .com taken
  - Kazi won on punchiness + global pronounceability; HeyKazi preserves it with available .com

## Landing Page
- **Headline**: "Practice management, built for Africa" (or "Hey Kazi — practice management, built for Africa")
- **Tone**: Approachable + modern, emphasizing SA-specific parameters
- **CTAs**: "Book a Demo" (primary) + "Get Started" (secondary → admin-approved provisioning)
- **Pricing**: "Contact for pricing" — too early to publish specific numbers
- **Removed**: Fake logos, fake testimonials, fake stats
- **Added**: "Built for South Africa" positioning section (FICA, POPIA, ZAR, SA workflows)
- **4 Stories**: Time → Billing → Compliance → Visibility (with screenshot placeholders for Phase 53)
