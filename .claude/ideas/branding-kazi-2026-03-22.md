# Branding Decision — Kazi
**Date**: 2026-03-22

## Product Name: Kazi
- **Origin**: Swahili for "work" — pan-African, meaningful, 4 letters
- **Domains**: kazi.com (primary), kazi.africa (brand/email), kazi.co.za (purchased by someone, no site)
- **Rejected alternatives**:
  - Sebenza (Zulu: work) — Chris Reeve Knives brand collision, SA startup namespace crowded
  - Lumino (Latin: light) — clean but not African
  - Khanya (Xhosa: shine) — beautiful but khanya.co.za active site
  - Vutha, Modiri, Kazi all considered; Kazi won on punchiness + global pronounceability

## Landing Page
- **Headline**: "Practice management, built for Africa"
- **Tone**: Approachable + modern, emphasizing SA-specific parameters
- **CTAs**: "Book a Demo" (primary) + "Get Started" (secondary → admin-approved provisioning)
- **Pricing**: "Contact for pricing" — too early to publish specific numbers
- **Removed**: Fake logos, fake testimonials, fake stats
- **Added**: "Built for South Africa" positioning section (FICA, POPIA, ZAR, SA workflows)
- **4 Stories**: Time → Billing → Compliance → Visibility (with screenshot placeholders for Phase 53)

## Future: AI-Powered Demo Provisioning
Founder's vision (deferred): "Tell me about your firm" intake → AI generates demo tenant with firm name, sample data, custom screenshots, and guided walkthrough. All infrastructure exists (Phase 39 provisioning, Phase 49 vertical profiles, seed data). Phase 55+ candidate.

Landing page uses Signal Deck design system: dark hero (slate-950), teal-500/600 accents, Sora headlines, IBM Plex Sans body. In-repo at `app/page.tsx` + `components/marketing/`.
