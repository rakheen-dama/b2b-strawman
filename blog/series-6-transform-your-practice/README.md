# Series 6: "Run a Leaner Practice with Kazi"

Marketing-oriented blog series for professional services firm owners. Not technical — outcome-focused.

**Product name**: Kazi (not DocTeams)
**Audience**: Partners, directors, and practice managers at 3-20 person accounting, law, and consulting firms in South Africa.
**Tone**: Empathetic, practical, peer-to-peer. Written as if by someone who understands the daily reality of running a small firm — the late invoicing, the FICA scramble before inspections, the "which clients are actually making us money" question nobody has time to answer.

## Publishing

- Platform: Kazi product blog (kazi.co.za/blog or similar)
- Cross-post to LinkedIn (SA professional services audience)
- Frequency: Weekly for 6 weeks at launch
- Each post ends with a soft CTA (request demo / join waitlist)

## Tone Guide

- Use "you" and "your firm" — speak directly to the reader
- Use SA terminology: engagements (not projects), clients (not customers), SARS, FICA, IRBA
- Use ZAR examples (R1,500/hour, R40,000 monthly revenue)
- Acknowledge the reality: small firms are busy, under-resourced, and skeptical of software promises
- No jargon: no "multi-tenant", no "schema isolation", no "ScopedValue"
- Show, don't sell: describe outcomes, not features
