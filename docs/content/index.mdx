# HeyKazi Documentation

Welcome to the HeyKazi documentation. HeyKazi is a professional services management
platform built for accounting and legal firms. Manage your projects, track time,
invoice clients, and streamline your practice — all from one place.

Browse the guides below to get started, explore features, or configure your workspace.
