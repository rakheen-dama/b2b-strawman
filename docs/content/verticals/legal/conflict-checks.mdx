# Conflict Checks

<Callout type="info">
  This feature is coming soon. The sections below describe planned capabilities.
</Callout>

## What's Planned

Conflict checks will allow law firms to screen new matters against existing clients and opposing parties before accepting an engagement. When you open a new matter, the system will search across your client database, matter records, and associated parties to flag potential conflicts of interest. Results will be presented in a clear report showing the nature of each potential conflict, the related matter, and the parties involved.

Planned features include configurable conflict rules (for example, different thresholds for direct representation versus peripheral involvement), the ability to record a conflict waiver with supporting documentation, and an audit trail of every check performed — essential for Law Society compliance.

## What's Available Today

While the dedicated conflict check feature is in development, you can use the following features as a manual workaround:

- [Customers](/features/customers) — search your client database by name, registration number, or custom fields to identify existing relationships.
- [Projects](/features/projects) — review active and completed matters to check for overlapping parties.
- [Documents & Templates](/features/documents) — store conflict waiver letters and related correspondence.
