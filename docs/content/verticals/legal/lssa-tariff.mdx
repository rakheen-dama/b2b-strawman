# LSSA Tariff Billing

<Callout type="info">
  This feature is coming soon. The sections below describe planned capabilities.
</Callout>

## What's Planned

LSSA tariff billing will provide law firms with built-in support for billing according to the Law Society of South Africa tariff guidelines. You will be able to select tariff items when recording time or creating invoices, with rates automatically applied based on the current published tariff. The feature will support both attorney-client billing (the fee your client pays) and party-party billing (recoverable costs from the opposing party), with separate rate schedules for each.

Planned features include a tariff rate library that is updated with each published tariff revision, the ability to generate detailed bills of costs for taxation proceedings, and support for tariff-based fee estimates at matter inception. The system will distinguish between High Court and Magistrate's Court tariffs where applicable.

## What's Available Today

While dedicated LSSA tariff support is in development, you can use the following features to manage legal billing:

- [Time Tracking](/features/time-tracking) -- record time entries against matters with hourly rates.
- [Invoicing](/features/invoicing) -- create invoices with custom line items for tariff-based charges.
- [Rate Cards](/features/rate-cards) -- configure custom rate structures that approximate tariff rates for your most common activities.
- [Projects](/features/projects) -- track matter budgets and compare actual costs against estimates.
