- Each reviewer's brief: "find the slop, not correctness." Specifically:
  - Tests claiming PASS with limited scope (`-Dtest='*Foo*'` instead of `mvnw verify`)

  - AI smells in the changed files: excessive comments, inconsistent naming with surrounding code, redundant abstractions, mixed idioms
