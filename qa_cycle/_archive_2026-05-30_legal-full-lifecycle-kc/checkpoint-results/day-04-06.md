### New Gap: OBS-5004 — Trust field group auto-attached to non-trust clients
- **Severity**: MEDIUM
- **Description**: When creating a new client with entity type PTY_LTD, the system automatically attaches both "SA Accounting — Client Details" AND "SA Accounting — Trust Details" field groups. The trust field group has 3 required fields (Trust Registration Number, Trust Deed Date, Trust Type) which are irrelevant for a company client. This causes "Required Fields: 2/5" status in Client Readiness. Workaround: manually remove the Trust Details field group via the X button on the FIELD GROUPS section of the client detail page.
- **Day**: 4
- **Non-cascading**: Yes — workaround available, does not block onboarding transition.
