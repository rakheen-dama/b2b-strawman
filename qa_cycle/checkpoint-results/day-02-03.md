## New GAPs Identified

### GAP-P49-016 (major): Clauses not rendered in document body
**Severity**: Major
**Templates affected**: All 3 engagement letter templates (bookkeeping, tax-return, advisory)
**Description**: The clause selection step (Step 1 of 2) in the generation dialog allows users to select/deselect clauses, but the selected clauses are NOT rendered in the final document HTML. The document body goes from "Client Responsibilities" / "Acceptance" straight to the footer with no clause sections.
**Expected**: Selected clauses (Limitation of Liability, Termination, Confidentiality, Document Retention, etc.) should appear as labeled sections in the document body.
**Impact**: Engagement letters are legally incomplete without clause content. All clause-bearing templates are affected.
**Track**: T1 (Template Variable Fidelity), T2 (Clause Assembly -- preview of Day 4 finding)

### GAP-P49-017 (minor): Engagement type renders as raw enum instead of display label
**Severity**: Minor
**Templates affected**: engagement-letter-bookkeeping
**Description**: `project.customFields.engagement_type` renders as "MONTHLY_BOOKKEEPING" (raw enum/slug) instead of "Monthly Bookkeeping" (display label). The custom field dropdown on the project page shows "Monthly Bookkeeping" as the selected label, but the template receives the raw value.
**Expected**: Human-readable label should appear in the document.
**Impact**: Cosmetic -- document is readable but unprofessional.

### GAP-P49-018 (minor): Invoice/statement table amounts lack currency formatting
**Severity**: Minor
**Templates affected**: invoice-za (line items table), statement-of-account (invoice history table)
**Description**: Amounts in template tables render as raw numbers (e.g., "5500.00", "6497.50") without the R prefix or SA thousand-separator formatting (R 5 500,00). The Amount Summary section of the invoice template correctly uses "R5 500,00" format, making the formatting inconsistent within the same document. Dates in statement tables also show raw ISO (2026-03-17) instead of human-readable format.
**Expected**: Consistent currency formatting (R X XXX,XX) and date formatting (DD Month YYYY) throughout.
**Impact**: Documents look inconsistent. Not blocking but unprofessional.

## Expanded Scope of GAP-P49-013

**Original**: "Naledi project not linked to customer"
**Updated finding**: The issue is wider than originally documented. Of the 7 projects in the system:
- **Monthly Bookkeeping -- Kgosi**: HAS customer link (only one that works fully)
- **Annual Tax Return 2026 -- Kgosi**: Shows "Internal Project", NO customer link from project side (but IS linked from customer side)
- **BEE Certificate Review -- Vukani**: Shows "Internal Project", NO customer link
- **Monthly Bookkeeping -- Naledi**: Shows "Internal Project" (previously documented)
- **Monthly Bookkeeping -- Vukani**: Not tested but likely same issue
- **Annual Administration -- Moroka Trust**: Not tested but likely same issue

**Impact**: 5 of 6 client projects cannot generate engagement letters with correct customer data. Only the "Monthly Bookkeeping -- Kgosi" project produces complete documents. This is a seed data issue -- the project-to-customer link is established from the customer page but not bidirectionally available to the template rendering context.

**Recommendation**: Elevate GAP-P49-013 from major to **blocker** for engagement letter testing. The seed script needs to set the `customerId` field on each project, not just link projects from the customer side.
